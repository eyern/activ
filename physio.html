About Us

Welcome to Active Physiotherapy Clinic

A leading healthcare facility in Kiambu, Nairobi, Kenya. We offer a comprehensive range of therapies designed to improve the overall health and wellbeing of our patients.

Our Physiotherapits are licensed by Physiotherapy Council of Kenya

Our team of experienced and highly skilled therapists are dedicated to providing personalized and compassionate care to every patient that walks through our doors.
We understand that every individual is unique, and we take the time to listen to our patients’ concerns and goals before developing a customized treatment plan to meet their specific needs.

Our Physiotherapy Services

Heat Therapy

Heat therapy, or thermotherapy, is a standard physiotherapy service used to alleviate chronic pain, reduce muscle stiffness, and accelerate healing by increasing blood flow to targeted areas

Myofascial Release

Myofascial Release (MFR) is a specialized manual therapy that uses gentle, sustained pressure to release tension in the fascia—the connective tissue surrounding muscles and organs

Electro Therapy

Electrotherapy is a non-invasive physiotherapy treatment that uses mild electrical impulses to stimulate nerves and muscles, primarily to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, and accelerate healing

Medical Cupping

a specialized therapeutic technique used in physiotherapy to treat musculoskeletal pain, improve circulation, and enhance muscle recovery. Unlike massage, which uses downward pressure, cupping uses negative pressure (suction) to lift skin and muscle fibers, encouraging blood flow to target

Chiropractor

Focuses on spinal alignment and nervous system function through manual adjustments, physiotherapy (or physical therapy) targets muscles, tendons, and soft tissues through exercises and rehabilitation.

Osteopathy

Osteopathy can first relieve immediate tension and improve joint mobility through hands-on adjustments.

Sports Physiotherapy

Active Physiotherapy Clinic Offers a variety of specialized sports physiotherapy services ranging from high-performance athletic rehabilitation to convenient home-based recovery

Orthopaedic Manual Therapy

We use hands-on techniques like joint mobilization and soft-tissue manipulation to treat musculoskeletal conditions.

Occupation Therapy

Focuses on restoring physical movement, strength, and mobility, occupational therapy centers on helping individuals regain independence in performing daily tasks (occupations) such as dressing, eating, and working

Speech Therapy

We specialize in a wide range of rehabilitation, including speech therapy for communication improvement and physiotherapy for injury recovery and pain management.

Acupuncture Therapy

Involves inserting thin needles into specific body points to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, and improve function, often as part of a comprehensive rehabilitation plan. It is widely used for musculoskeletal issues like back pain, arthritis, and nerve-related pain, often accelerating recovery when combined with manual therapy and exercise

Home-based Physiotherapy services

Home-based physiotherapy brings professional rehabilitation directly to your home, offering a convenient alternative to hospital visits, especially for those with limited mobility, chronic pain, or busy schedules

Pelvic Health & Biofeedback

Focuses on rehabilitating disorders of the pelvic floor, which can cause issues with bladder, bowel, and sexual function. Biofeedback is a specialized tool used during this therapy that uses sensors to provide real-time visual or auditory cues, helping you see how your muscles are contracting or relaxing on a screen.
